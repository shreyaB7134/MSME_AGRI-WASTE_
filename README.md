# MSME_AGRI-WASTE_
Agri Cycle is an offline-first B2B platform turning farm waste into rural micro-enterprises. It uses on-device AI (TensorFlow Lite) to grade biomass quality without internet. When connected, a cloud ML engine matches inventory with industrial buyers, optimizing green supply chains and stopping crop burning.


# 🌱 AgriCycle – Turning Agricultural Waste into Wealth

> AI-powered platform that transforms agricultural waste into profitable business opportunities for farmers, SHGs, MSMEs, and rural entrepreneurs.

---

# 📖 About

AgriCycle is an AI-powered mobile application designed to reduce agricultural waste burning by helping users identify agricultural waste and convert it into value-added products.

The platform provides:

- AI-based waste identification
- Product recommendations
- DIY tutorials
- Marketplace for buying and selling agri-waste
- Government scheme information
- Business guidance for rural entrepreneurs

Our mission is to promote sustainable farming while creating income opportunities through circular economy practices.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

India generates millions of tons of agricultural waste every year.

Major challenges include:

- Crop residue burning
- Environmental pollution
- Lack of awareness
- No structured marketplace
- Limited access to business knowledge

Farmers often dispose of valuable waste because they do not know its commercial value.

AgriCycle solves this problem using Artificial Intelligence.

---

# 💡 Proposed Solution

AgriCycle allows users to:

1. Capture or upload an image of agricultural waste.
2. AI identifies the waste type.
3. Recommendation engine suggests possible products.
4. User learns manufacturing process through tutorials.
5. Marketplace connects sellers and buyers.
6. Farmers generate additional income.
